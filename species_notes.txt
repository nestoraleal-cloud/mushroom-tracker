OYSTER MUSHROOM (Pleurotus ostreatus)
--------------------------------------
Difficulty: Very Beginner Friendly
Substrate: Wheat straw, coffee grounds, cardboard
Temperature: 15-24°C
Humidity: 85-95%
Notes: Great beginner mushroom, fast colonizer
